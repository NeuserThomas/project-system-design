Normal flow to be followed. 1. You arrive at the parking and can see how much parking spots are left. 2. If there are still parking spots left, you get a new parking ticket. 3. Remember the ID of the generated parking ticket as you will need this later on. (Normally this is included in the barcode) 4. You go to the ticketservice and buy a ticket for a movie. Remember the id of the cinema ticket that will be generated as you will need this when validating your parking ticket. (Normally included in the barcode) 5. After the movie, you go to the parking machine to validate your parkingticket with your cinema ticket. 6. After succesfully validating your parking ticket, you can now exit the parking.
When you click a button and nothing happens, please check the network panel. Probably an network call returned an error that will then be specified in the response body. Hover over the buttons to get some more info.
